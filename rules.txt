INSTRUCTIONS AND RULES FOR TIC TAC TOE:
1. The game is played on a square grid of n x n dimension where n should be greater than or equal to 3.

2. You are X, your friend is O. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.

3. You are required to choose a position that isn't already taken and doesn't exceed the dimension.

4. You can choose your own symbols instead of the conventional X and O in this game.

5. The first player to get all marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.

6. When all the squares are full, the game is over. If no player has all marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.

7. The performance of the payers will be stored in a file for the players to view at the end of the game.
